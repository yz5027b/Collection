COOPER HEWITT COLLECTION

ORIENTAL PLATE, CA. 1878–85

This is a Plate. It is dated ca. 1878–85 and we acquired it in 1999. Its medium is glazed earthenware. It is a part of the Product Design and Decorative Arts department.

This object was donated by Brooklyn Museum. It is credited Gift of the Brooklyn Museum of Art, Paul F. Walter Collection.

Its dimensions are H x diam.: 2.5 x 24.2 cm (1 in. x 9 1/2 in.)

This object was bequest of Elinor Merrell. It is credited Bequest of Elinor Merrell.
