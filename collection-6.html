COOPER HEWITT COLLECTION

SAMPLE (USA), 1935–71

This is a Sample. It was designed by Mary Dumas. It is dated 1935–71 and we acquired it in 1974. Its medium is cotton and its technique is resist dyed. It is a part of the Textiles department.

Its dimensions are H x W: 69.9 x 27.9 cm (27 1/2 x 11 in.)
